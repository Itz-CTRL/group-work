Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update my profile information?
Navigate to your profile page from the dashboard, click on "Edit Profile", make your changes, and click "Save Changes". Your information will be updated immediately.
How can I reset my password?
Go to Settings from your dashboard, enter your current password, then enter and confirm your new password. Click "Change Password" to complete the process.
How do I access the alumni directory?
Click on "Alumni Directory" from your dashboard. You can search for alumni by name, graduation year, or location. Make sure your profile is complete to appear in searches.
Can I make a donation to the alumni fund?
Yes! Visit the "Give Back / Donate" page from your dashboard. You can choose from various donation tiers and specify how you'd like your contribution to be used.
How do I register for alumni events?
Navigate to the "Events" section from your dashboard. Browse upcoming events and click "Register" on any event you'd like to attend. You'll receive a confirmation email with event details.
How can I subscribe to the alumni newsletter?
Visit the "Newsletter" page and enter your email address in the subscription form. You can also manage your subscription preferences from your Settings page.
What career resources are available?
The "Career Opportunities" section features job postings, internships, and mentorship programs. You can also connect with alumni in your field of interest through the directory.
Is my personal information secure?
Yes, we take data security seriously. All personal information is encrypted and stored securely. You can control your privacy settings from your Settings page.
